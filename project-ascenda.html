Ascenda.

Ascenda is an original app designed to help women overcome impostor syndrome in their careers — the persistent feeling of being a fraud despite real achievements, competence and hard-earned success. We built the full brand experience, from visual identity and UX/UI to campaign, merchandise and out-of-home, creating a system that feels both bold and deeply human.
